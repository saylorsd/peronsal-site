I mostly make my living building things with folks in my community to help us leverage open data.

I also make things with researchers, providing my skills in data communication and application development.

Below is a sample of some of my professional work.

  • Parcels N'at

    Parcels N'at provides a single interface to access a diverse set of property and housing related datasets across Allegheny County. Users can explore data parcel-by-parcel, see region-level indicators of aggregate parcel data, and filter, join and download parcel data in bulk.
